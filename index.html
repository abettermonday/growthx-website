How I work

Understanding the foundation first

Before anything else, I need to understand what's actually going on.

That means looking at your sales process, how your team runs calls and demos, how you're messaging to market, and where deals are getting stuck. Not to audit you — just to understand the foundation before we do anything useful with it.

Most of the time there are a few things working well, a few clear gaps, and a picture of what good looks like for a company at your stage. That's where we start.

What happens next

Hands-on, not hand-off

Once we know what good looks like, I help you get there. That might mean redesigning a part of your process, working directly with your team on calls and demos, tightening your messaging, or some combination of all three.

The work is hands-on. I'm not handing over a deck and leaving.
